Project 2

Exquisite Corpse

Michelle & Yingying

For our project, we decided to make different parts of the face and have them come together as one. My creation is the left half of the face. I first sketched a version of my idea digitally on my iPad before I brought the images in to Adobe Illustrator and recreated it with vector illustration.

We established base rules to follow. Throughout the process, we shared our progress and made changes so that our face would line up.

Rules

- 2x2 grid (600 x 600px squares)

- Line art inspired

- No color limitations
